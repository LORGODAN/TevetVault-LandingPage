How to install on Android

Takes less than 2 minutes

1
Download the APKTap the green Download App button at the top of this page.
2
Allow installationGo to Settings → Security → turn on "Install from unknown sources" or tap "Allow" when your phone asks.
3
Open the APK fileOpen your Downloads folder, tap the TevetVault file and tap Install.
4
Register and startOpen TevetVault, create your free account and start accessing materials immediately.
Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about TevetVault

Yes — TevetVault is 100% free for all students. You can register, browse, view, download and upload materials without paying anything. It will always be free.

Publishing an app on Google Play Store requires a $25 developer registration fee. Since TevetVault is a free community project built for Malawi students, the APK is distributed directly so that no fees are involved. You can install it safely by downloading the APK from this page.

Yes, the APK is completely safe. TevetVault is a web app wrapped into an Android package. It does not ask for unnecessary permissions and does not collect any personal data beyond what you provide during registration (email, name, course and college).

Yes, TevetVault requires an internet connection to load and download materials. Once you download a file to your phone you can read it offline. We recommend using WiFi when downloading large files to save your mobile data.

The APK download is for Android phones only. However TevetVault also works as a website that you can open in any browser on iPhone, laptop or desktop computer. Just tap "View in Browser" and it works on any device.

Any registered student can upload materials. We encourage all students to share their notes and past papers to help others. Materials can be reported if they contain wrong or inappropriate content and admin will review and remove them.

Currently TevetVault supports ICT, Plumbing, Carpentry, Bricklaying, Automobile Mechanics and Tailoring. All with Level 1 to Level 3 materials. More courses can be added by the admin. If your course is not listed contact us via WhatsApp.
